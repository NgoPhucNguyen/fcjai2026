### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is very friendly and open. Members at FCJ are always willing to help when I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is neat and comfortable, helping me stay focused.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor provides very detailed guidance. The admin team supports procedures, documentation, and creates favorable conditions for me to work effectively.

**3. Alignment Between Work and Academic Major**  
The tasks assigned to me align well with the knowledge I have learned at university, while also expanding into new areas I had not been exposed to before. This allows me to both strengthen my foundational knowledge and gain practical skills.

**4. Learning Opportunities & Skill Development**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools, teamwork skills, and professional communication in a corporate environment. The mentor also shared practical experiences that helped me better orient my career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, works seriously while maintaining a friendly atmosphere. During urgent projects, everyone collaborates and supports each other regardless of position. This made me feel like part of the team, even as an intern.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**  
The company provides internship allowances and offers flexible working time when needed. Additionally, being able to participate in internal training sessions is a big advantage.
